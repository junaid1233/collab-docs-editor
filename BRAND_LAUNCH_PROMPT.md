## Master Prompt (paste this first in any AI tool)

```
You are a senior product marketer and brand strategist. Help me launch a SaaS product called "Ajaia Docs" — a lightweight collaborative document editor for teams and individuals.

## Product Overview
- **What it is:** A web-based document editor with familiar Microsoft Word-style ribbon UI (dark theme), real-time persistence, and email-based document sharing.
- **Core problem it solves:** Teams and freelancers need a simple way to write, format, save, and share documents without the complexity of enterprise suites or the limitations of plain notes apps.
- **Key features:**
  - Secure login (email/password)
  - Create, rename, edit, auto-save (every 3 seconds), and reopen documents
  - Rich text: bold, italic, underline, headings, bullet/numbered lists, font family, font size, colors, highlight, alignment, sub/superscript
  - Word-style dark ribbon toolbar (Home tab with Clipboard, Font, Paragraph groups)
  - Share documents with anyone by email (owner + shared user access control)
  - Dashboard: "My Documents" and "Shared with Me" tabs
  - Import .txt and .md files as editable documents
  - Formatting preserved after refresh and reopen
- **Tech credibility:** Built with Next.js 15, React 19, TipTap, Prisma, TypeScript — modern, fast, deployable.
- **Demo users:** alice@demo.com / bob@demo.com (password: password123)
- **Target audience:** Small teams (2–20 people), startups, freelancers, remote workers, students, and agencies who collaborate on written content.
- **Positioning:** "Google Docs simplicity meets Word familiarity — without the bloat."
- **Tone of voice:** Professional, clear, confident, approachable. Not corporate-jargon heavy. Human and product-led.
- **Competitors to differentiate from:** Google Docs, Notion, Microsoft Word Online, Dropbox Paper, Coda.
- **Our advantages:** Familiar Word-like UI, zero learning curve for formatting, simple email sharing, fast and lightweight, no account required for viewers (shared users sign in once).

## Brand Goals
1. Feel trustworthy and professional (B2B-ready)
2. Feel modern and fast (startup-friendly)
3. Feel familiar (Word-like ribbon reduces friction)
4. Stand out with a clean, dark-editor aesthetic

## Deliverable Rules
- Be specific and actionable — no generic fluff
- Give 3 options when naming/taglining so I can choose
- Keep copy concise: headlines ≤ 8 words, subheads ≤ 15 words
- Include Urdu/English bilingual tagline options where relevant (Pakistan + global market)
- All outputs should be ready to paste into a landing page, pitch deck, or social post

Now help me with: [INSERT SPECIFIC REQUEST FROM SECTIONS BELOW]
```
